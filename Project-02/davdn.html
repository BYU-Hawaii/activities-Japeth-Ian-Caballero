Welcome to Davao del Norte! This is where I spend my 6th transfer. Some places are just recovering from the pandemic, and this place is no exception. Davao del Norte is the province but I was assigned in Delfina branch and Mankilam branch of Tagum City, and Kapalong branch. Yes, I handled 3 branches simultaneously due to lack of missionaries. Despite that, I was assigned to a companion who I was close with before, and that made the work fun. After all, we did get to baptize two individuals that time.
