Welcome to Surigao del Norte! In this province lies my 3rd area, the town of Tubod. 'Tubod' means 'Spring' in Cebuano, which is why there are a lot of hidden springs in this town that I get to see when my companions and I go tracting. Did I say 'companions'? Oh yeah, I stayed in this town for 9 months, so I get to have 3 companions while serving in this area. I mean, this is where I got stuck when the pandemic came. Still, reminiscing about this town help me realize the tender mercies I receive from the Lord.
